The Knight

The Knight is the silent protagonist — a small, mysterious vessel who journeys through Hallownest. This entry contains lore, notable quotes, and links to related characters.

Hollow Knight

The Hollow Knight is the principal boss encounter in Hallownest: a towering, disciplined vessel consumed by infection. It challenges the player with precise, patterned attacks, rapid lunges, and a devastating second phase. Learning its timings and openings is key to victory — defeating the Hollow Knight resolves an important story thread and rewards the player with closure and unique rewards.

Soul Master

The Soul Master is a powerful sorcerer encountered in the Soul Sanctum. He telegraphs devastating soul attacks, uses hovering and rapid teleportation, and forces the player to time their jumps and dodges precisely. Learning to interrupt his casting windows is essential to surviving this encounter.

Mantis Lords

The Mantis Lords are a trio of elite mantis warriors whose duel tests the player's speed and reflexes. They trade precise, rhythmic strikes and close-range combos, rewarding players who can read their patterns and counterattack during openings.

Hornet

Hornet is a swift and agile foe who blends melee attacks with acrobatic maneuvers and ranged needle barrages. Encounters with Hornet emphasize mobility, parrying opportunities, and quick decision-making. Hornet also plays a major role in the game's narrative.

Broken Vessel

The Broken Vessel is a tragic, hollowed remnant of the ancient vessels. It attacks with rapid strikes and dashes, demonstrating both ferocity and erratic movement. The fight is challenging due to its speed and occasional unpredictable lunges.
