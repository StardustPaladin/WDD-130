Rivers

Discover rivers perfect for all experience levels. Below are a few of our favorite runs with brief descriptions and photos.

Dry Oar offers guided trips, family floats, and multi-day excursions. Each river has unique scenery and difficulty — read the short summaries below to find the trip that fits your group. For safety, bring weather-appropriate clothing, a personal flotation device if you have one, and follow your guide's instructions on guided trips.

Clearwater Run

Gentle stretches, family-friendly float trips, and scenic views. Ideal for beginners and families with younger children. Typical trip length: 2-3 hours.

Thunder Rapids

Exciting whitewater sections for thrill seekers. Guided trips available. Not recommended for first-time boaters without a guide — expect class III-IV rapids in places.

High Peak Gorge

Remote canyon runs with camping access; great for multi-day adventures. Perfect for experienced paddlers who want scenic campsites and long days on the water.

Trip Planning & Safety

We run trips year-round where conditions allow. Before you come, check weather and river levels, and contact us if you have special gear needs. Our guides carry first-aid kits and safety equipment for all guided runs.

To book a guided trip or ask about group rates, visit our Contact Us page or call the office.
